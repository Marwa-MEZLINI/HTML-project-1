Black Forrest Cake

Black Forest (Schwarzwälder Kirschtorte) is a German pastry, a cocoa sponge cake filled with whipped cream and cherries.

Ingredients
For the sponge cake For the whipped cream Cherries For the decor Equipment
  • 4 eggs , at room temperature
  • ½ cup caster sugar
  • ½ cup flour , sifted
  • ½ cup cornstarch (or potato starch)
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder , 100% cocoa
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Butter , for the mold
  • Flour , for the mold
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • A few drops vanilla extract
  • 8 oz. morello cherries in syrup keep 6 oz. of syrup for the syrup
  • 6 oz. canned sour cherry syrup
  • 3 tablespoons kirsch
  • Dark chocolate shavings
  • Electric whisk
  • Springform cake pan 8 inches / 20 cm in diameter
  • Culinary robot
  • Spatula
  • Pastry bag
  • Piping tip plain or fluted
